About Me

I am a senior at the University of St. Thomas earning my double major in computer science and computer engineering. In my engineering senior design program I am getting the opportunity to do design work for Polaris. In my computer science senior design course I am gaining experience in web development. This aligns with the experience I gained the past two summers working as a full-stack software engineering intern at Medtronic.

I am an active member of my University’s Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) chapter. I served as the Event Coordinator from October 2023 to May 2024 where I took over as President. Since taking over as President I have worked towards building community and hosting more frequent events. In my time as President I have increased average attendance by 107% and increased event frequency by 41%.

As part of my engineering studies at the University of St. Thomas I had the opportunity to study in Seoul, South Korea for a month. I was taking an engineering course called "High-Tech Manufacturing, Engineering, and Society". Through this I gained an understanding of global engineering and had the opportunity to tour manufacturing sites for Hyundai Motors, Hyundai Heavy Industry, LG, iLabs, and Telstar.

I am also an active member of the Society of Women Engineers (SWE). As a freshman in high school, I had the opportunity to attend the SWE conference for the first time. When I got to college I joined my University’s SWE board. As a board member, I had the opportunity to attend the SWE conference in Chicago last year and in New Orleans this year. This has given me the opportunity to network and gain community within the field of engineering.
